# Review Request

> A few days after an order ships, ask the customer how it went and invite a quick review.

## Kitchen

- Shopify
- Gmail

## Steps

### 1. Listen for a shipped order

*Card: Listen*

- Listen for: shipped order
- From: Shopify

### 2. Wait for it to arrive

*Card: Wait*

- How long: 5 days

### 3. Ask how it went

*Card: Send Message*

- To: {step 1: customer email}
- Subject: How was your order?
- Message: Your order should have arrived by now — we hope you love it! If you have a minute, we'd be grateful for a quick review. It helps our small shop more than you'd think.
