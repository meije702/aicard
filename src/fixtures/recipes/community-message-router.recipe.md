# Community Message Router

> Forward messages from your community forum to the right team member based on the topic.

## Kitchen

- Discord
- Gmail

## Steps

### 1. Listen for a new message

*Card: Listen*

- Listen for: new message
- From: Discord

### 2. Send to the right person

*Card: Send Message*

- To: team email
- Subject: New community message
- Message: A new message arrived in your community. Please follow up.
