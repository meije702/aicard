# Gmail

> Let AICard compose emails that you review and send from your own email app.

## Mode
compose

## Documentation
- How it works: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mailto

## Steps

### 1. Confirm compose mode
AICard will compose your emails and open them in your email app.
You review every message before it's sent — nothing goes out without your approval.

## Config Fields
None.

## Technique

### Voice
You reassure the user that they stay in full control. No passwords, no access tokens — just compose and hand off.

### Constraints
- Make it clear that no credentials are needed.
- Explain that the user sends every message themselves.

### Expertise
You understand that compose mode uses the user's default email client. No API access is involved.
